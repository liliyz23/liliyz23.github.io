AS IF

AS IF是一组反映＂不存在性＂的交互艺术装置。一束光打在旋转的空花瓶上，投射出的阴影里，有花束肆意绽放，与花瓶的影子相融，一同旋转，仿佛花瓶中真的有一束无形无质的花；当试探的手伸向虚无的花朵，连花影也会归于沉寂。不存在的花朵或许是未曾拥有者的渴望，或许是失去者久久不能忘怀的悔恨。究竟哪一个更令人感到空虚呢？ AS IF is a series of interactive installations with the aim of visualizing the nonexistence. Shadows of flowers are casted immediately above vases, merging with their own shadows. The rotating vases and shadows give the nonexistent flowers the sense of volume, but also deepen their hollowness. Should the viewer try to touch the nonexistent flowers, the shadows of the flowers will dissipate. The nonexistent flowers could be the long-lasting desire of “having” or the lingering regret of “losing”.
