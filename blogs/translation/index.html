Translation

翻译·化身是一个沉浸式交互装置，它探讨着数字形态下人体、算法、工具之间的关系。 互联网和智能应用的普及使得大量跨文化、跨语言的信息在数字世界中不断传递。 这个作品以AI翻译系统为主体，背景中是原文，人体的轮廓内是通过AI翻译得到的译文，隐喻着智能算法为人们打开一扇窗，是人的能力的延伸，虽然目前的精度差强人意，但随着技术的不断渗透和人们的持续情感参与，融合将成为常态。

Translation is an interactive and immersive installation which illustrates a dynamic system of communication between the human body and the digital interface. The internet and smart devices make a sheer quantity of data available and achievable. Cross-cultural and cross-language information continually transmit in the digital world. In this exhibition, viewers will interact with the magical translation system by their body movements. This exhibition is a metaphor for our ongoing emotional involvement with digital systems in our lived environment.
