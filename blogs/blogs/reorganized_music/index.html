Reorganized Music

A new way to manipulate sound and create unique effects

Reorganized Music uses a method called Granular Synthesis to redistribute and restructure fragments of music to produce sound effects and music. *Granular Synthesis is a powerful algorithm for manipulating sounds, to adjust many sound parameters such as speed and pitch. Each music fragment is a grain. By combining different grains in various ways, participants cand create unique sound effects.
